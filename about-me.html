About Me

Hello, my name is Toua Lor. I am 25 years old and currently a student in Correlation One's Software Development Cohort 3 course.I hold a Bachelor's Degree in Business Administration with a major in Business Information Systems and a minor in International Business. Currently I am employed as an Amazon Associate employee while studying software development. I am looking forward to graduation from Correlation One and becoming a web or software developer.

My journey to becoming a software and web developer

My journey in web and software development actually started when I was in college. As part of my major in Business Information Systems, I was required to take some software development courses and actually found myself really liking it. Although my stint with learning about software and web developer was quite short and only surface knowledge level, I still thorougly enjoyed it. After graduation I was not able to find any job and ended up at Amazon. After a few years I learned about Amazon's career choice and decided that I wanted to go back to school and focus on software developement in the hopes of landing a better job.

So far my journey in Correlation One's Software developement course has been nothing but phenemonal. We have learned about the basics and advanced elements in HTML and it has been really informative and fun creating and working on the difference exercises. My favorite thing so far is working in Codio. Being able to see right away how your code transitions into the web page has been really cool and eye opening and I am looking forward to learning even more. Another major thing I am most excited for is working with my Career success coach in the hopes of finding and landing a job in software or web development when this course is over.
